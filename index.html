About the Platform

Promoting Cleanliness,
Environmental Awareness
& Sustainable Living

This website is developed to promote cleanliness, environmental awareness, and sustainable living through the use of modern technology. It serves as an interactive platform where users can monitor, report, and improve the cleanliness of their surroundings.

The website provides a smart dashboard that displays real-time data related to cleanliness levels, waste management, and environmental conditions. Users can easily report unclean areas, garbage dumping, or pollution issues, helping authorities and communities take quick action.

It also focuses on spreading awareness about eco-friendly practices by sharing useful tips and guidelines for reducing waste, saving resources, and adopting a greener lifestyle.

By encouraging active participation, the website aims to support initiatives like Swachhta and LiFE, creating a sense of responsibility among citizens toward maintaining a clean and healthy environment.
